About Me

Hello! My name is Wyatt Murray, and I'm a passionate Game Developer with a strong focus on C# and Unity, specializing in VR programming. I have 1 year of professional experience in VR development since graduating, complemented by 3 years of overall coding and experimentation in Unity, including my time in college. I thrive on crafting immersive game experiences and developing robust systems.

At Style Studios, I've served as an Assistant Programming Lead, overseeing feature integration and mentoring junior developers. As a Gameplay Programmer, I've implemented networking solutions using Unity Netcode for GameObjects, enhanced performance through Unity ECS design (achieving minimal FPS cost on Quest 2), and designed scalable inventory systems. My work also includes creating in-game tools to streamline design workflows.

My independent project, Project VRS, allowed me to develop an immersive VR spacecraft flight control system with dual-joystick functionality and real-time subsystem management. I collaborated on a physics-simulated flight system and designed dynamic combat environments with customizable missions. This project significantly enhanced my proficiency in rigidbody systems, vector math, event-driven programming, and component design.

I'm currently looking for new opportunities in game development roles, collaborations on interesting game dev projects, and opportunities to learn from and contribute to the programming community. In my spare time, I enjoy VR gaming, 3D printing, leatherwork, and homelab building & management with CasaOS.
